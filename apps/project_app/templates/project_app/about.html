About

Just Camping started with one person, an idea, and the love of camping. There are not that many resources online to give people detailed information about different camping spots. At Just Camping, we wanted to have a platform where a camping lover can book their favorite camping spot with ease.

We started with California because there are so many amazing places to explore, and of course we are from California. We want others to share our love of the beautiful places available to visit, even just for a day trip.

Keep a look out for the ULTIMATE camping blog, coming soon!
